### 👋 About

Network Analyst on the **WAN Operations** team at **Shared Services Canada** — ~2.5 years resolving escalated WAN faults across a multi-vendor **Cisco IOS / NX-OS** and **Juniper Junos** estate, plus asset lifecycle for **50,000+ networking devices** across federal departments. I live in **BGP, MPLS/L3VPN, and VXLAN-EVPN** by day, and turn the painful parts into tooling by night.

- 🔭 Building an **Infrastructure Digital Twin** and a streaming **network telemetry / observability** platform
- 🧰 My pattern: ship the tool **for my own Proxmox homelab first**, then scale it outward
- 🎓 **B.IT, Network Technology (Co-op)** — Carleton University & Algonquin College *(2028, A−)*; CCNA-track coursework complete
- 🔐 Active **Secret** clearance • **Bilingual EN/FR**
- 💬 Ask me about network automation, config drift, or making Ansible safe for non-engineers
